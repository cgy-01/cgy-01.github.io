About

I am an undergraduate student in the Remote Sensing Science and Technology program at the School of Remote Sensing and Geomatics Engineering, Nanjing University of Information Science and Technology (NUIST).

My interests lie in geospatial data science, with current research focused on intelligent remote sensing image analysis and the Google Earth Engine (GEE) cloud platform. I build open-source tools and write about what I learn.

Here are my latest technical updates and open-source code. I also share my learning journey across online communities. Looking forward to exchanging ideas with you.
