Writing, building, and thinking at the frontier of AI.

I am an AI-native creator and frontier blogger. I explore how large language models change the way we write, code, and think. This is where I share what I learn along the way.

I believe we are living through a shift in how creative work gets done. AI is not a replacement for human thought. It is a new medium, a new toolset, a new way to explore ideas at speed. My work sits at the intersection of frontier model capabilities, prompt design, and practical AI workflows.

I write about what works and what does not. I test models, build tools, break prompts, and share the results. No hype, no hand-waving. Just honest exploration of what these systems can and cannot do, and what that means for the people who build with them.
